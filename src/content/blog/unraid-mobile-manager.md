## Why Do You Need Unraid Deck?

### 1. Native Experience Built for iOS

There are many third-party management tools out there, but most are just simple web wrappers packed into an App, or built roughly with cross-platform frameworks. Unraid Deck is different; it strictly follows the Apple Human Interface Guidelines (HIG).

The moment you open the App, it feels incredibly familiar. From fluid transitions to subtle Haptic Feedback, every detail has been polished with care.

The **Dashboard** is no longer a cold pile of data. We've transformed key metrics like CPU load, RAM usage, and array storage into intuitive, elegant visualizations. With **System-level Dark Mode** support, checking your server status late at night is easy on the eyes.

### 2. "God Mode" at Your Fingertips

Containers (Docker) and Virtual Machines (VM) are the soul of Unraid, and also where things go wrong most often.

In Unraid Deck, managing them becomes a pleasure.
Imagine the next time Plex freezes, you just need to:
1. Pull out your phone and unlock with FaceID instantly.
2. Spot the container acting up.
3. **Swipe left gently** and tap "Restart".

The whole process takes less than 3 seconds. No tedious login, no painful zooming. This sense of "control" is something you only understand once you've tried it.

### 3. Security is the Bottom Line

I know that trusting server management rights to an App is a high cost.

Therefore, Unraid Deck adopts the purest **API Direct Connection mode**.
*   **Zero Relay**: Your phone communicates directly with your server; data NEVER passes through any third-party intermediate servers.
*   **Zero Collection**: We do not collect your domain, IP, or any server configuration information.
*   **Biometric Security**: Supports Face ID / Touch ID lock, so even if you lend your phone, you don't have to worry about someone accidentally deleting your 40TB treasure trove.
